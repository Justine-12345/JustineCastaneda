I'm Justine Castaneda, an enthusiastic upcoming BSIT graduate from the Technological University of the Philippines in Taguig. As I approach the completion of my degree on November 3, 2023, my excitement to dive deeper into the world of technology and programming grows. Proficient in various programming languages and web development, I find joy in meticulously crafting code and creating user-friendly applications.
